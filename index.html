Our Story

More Than a Diner

For 25 years, Missy's Arcade Restaurant has been the heartbeat of Titusville — the kind of place where your coffee cup never runs empty and your waitress remembers your name.

Owner Missy Wescoat opened the doors on March 5, 2001, and hasn't slowed down since. The walls are lined with Coca-Cola memorabilia, vintage neon, and enough nostalgia to make you forget what century it is. The signature pink interior? That's all Missy.

In 2018, a basement fire nearly took everything. Nine fire departments saved the block, and Missy rebuilt — every pink wall, every Coke sign, every booth. Because Titusville needed its bright spot back.

"We may look full, but tables turn over fast. We're worth the wait."

What We're Known For

The Good Stuff

Our menu changes with the seasons, but these are the things people drive across town for.

Legendary Breakfast

Pancakes, omelets, stack-it-up platters, and more. Served every morning starting at 6 AM. Too many favorites to list — just add a pancake.

Homemade Pies

Baked fresh. The kind your grandma used to make. Get a slice with ice cream or take a whole pie home — if there's any left.

Friday Fish Fry

Fish sandwiches and full fish dinners every Friday. A Titusville tradition. Get here early — they go fast.

🍔

Burgers & Melts

Handmade burgers, patty melts, and Missy's Monster Burger. Served with chips or add fries and coleslaw for the full experience.

🍲

Homemade Soups

Made from scratch, changes daily. Grab a cup or a bowl — or pair it with a half sandwich for the perfect lunch.

Kiddy Plates

Hotdog, grilled cheese, or chicken strip — served with fries. Because the little ones deserve a seat at the counter too.
